Percentage of coverage by Natura 2000

Member States were asked to report the area of the habitat which is covered by the Natura 2000 network. In some cases this information has been estimated using a different methodology to the estimate of overall area and this can lead to percentage covers greater than 100%. In such case the value has been given as 100% and highlighted with an asterisk (*). ‘na’ indicates that the MS has not reported the habitat area and/or the coverage by Natura 2000. No information is available for Greece.
