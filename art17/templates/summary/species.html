Species assessments at EU biogeographical level

Assessments on the conservation status of the habitat types and species of Community interest have been carried out in EU25 for the period 2001-2006 and in EU 27 for the period 2007-2012, compiled as part of the Habitats Directive - Article 17 reporting process. The data summary sheet for species conservation status provides an overview per biogeographical region. Once a selection has been made the conservation status can be visualised in a map view. Choose a period, a group, then a species belonging to that group. Optionally, further refine your query by selecting one of the available biogeographical regions for that species.
