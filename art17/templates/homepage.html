Online report on Article 17 of the Habitats Directive

Welcome to the Article 17 Web Tool on biogeographical assessments of conservation status for species and habitats, as reported by Member States according to the provisions of Article 17 of the Habitats Directive.

The MS assessments as well as the biogeographical assessments performed by the ETC/BD are now available for public view.

UNDER CONSTRUCTION: this site is currently being adapted to become the web-based Technical Report of Article 17.

The consultation is open only for users that will register. To register:

  • If you have an Eionet account please register here.
  • If you don't have an Eionet account please register here.
  • If you are a National Data Coordinator for Article 17, you are already registered.

There is no need to register if you just want to view the data. You just click on one of the links below. If you want as well to indicate how the assessments should be changed, than you will need to register.
