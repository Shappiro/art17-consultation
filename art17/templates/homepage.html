Online report on Article 17 of the Habitats Directive

Welcome to the Article 17 web tool on biogeographical assessments of conservation status of species and habitats under Article 17 of the Habitats Directive.

The biogeographical assessments from the Member States are now available for public view; the EU biogeographical assessments performed by the EEA and the ETC/BD are under development and will become available for public view and consultation during the second quarter of 2014.
